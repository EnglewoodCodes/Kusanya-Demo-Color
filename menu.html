About Our Food

Our mission at Kusanya is to gather our community together - and we believe that one of the best ways to accomplish that goal is through good food. We have worked hard to create unique menu items and hand-crafted drinks that are on par with the best coffee shops in the city. From savory lunch sandwiches to real fruit smoothies, our items reflect the diverse tastes of our neighborhood, using quality, fresh ingredients at prices which are accessible to all.
